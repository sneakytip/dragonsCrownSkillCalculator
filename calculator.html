Dragon's Crown Skill Calculator

Instructions

  1. Select a character class from the drop-down list. This will populate the class and common skill grids.
  2. In the skill grids, select any combination of desired skills and skill levels by clicking the increment/decrement arrows next to the input box under the Level column. The available number of skill points will automatically change with your selections.

Information

  • The Effects column will display the effect(s) of the skill at its current level towards the top of the cell.
  • The Effects column will display the effect(s) of the skill at its next level towards the bottom of the cell.
  • The Purchase Conditions column will display the conditions to purchase the next level of the skill.
